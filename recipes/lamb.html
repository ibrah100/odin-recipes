Lamp Chops with Garlic and Olive Oil

Description

A simple but delicious meat entree.

Ingredients

  • 6 Tbsp. olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 tsp. salt
  • 1/4 tsp. fresh-ground black pepper
  • 8 lamb rib chops, about 1 inch think (about 2 3/4 lbs. in all)

Steps

  1. Light the grill or heat the broiler. In a shallow dish, combine 4 tablespoons of the oil with the garlic, salt, and pepper. Add the lamb chops and turn to coat.
  2. Grill over high heat or broil the lamb chops for 5 minutes, basting with the remaining 2 tablespoons oil. Turn and cook until done, about 5 minutes longer.
